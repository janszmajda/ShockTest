# ShockTest Demo Script

**Total time: ~3 minutes**

---

## COLD OPEN — The Hook (0:00–0:20)

> "Prediction markets are supposed to be efficient. But we found something interesting."
>
> *[Show the dashboard headline stat — 59.9% reversion rate]*
>
> "We analyzed over 1,300 probability shocks across 107 Polymarket markets. Nearly 60% of them reverse within 6 hours. That's not random — that's a z-score of 7.13. **Markets overreact, and the overreaction is predictable.**"
>
> "We built ShockTest to detect it, explain it, and trade it."

---

## ACT 1 — The Dashboard (0:20–0:55)

> *[Show the main page with the featured shocks carousel]*
>
> "This is ShockTest. Every card is a real shock detected in the last few days. This one — *[click a political shock]* — jumped 15 percentage points in under an hour."
>
> *[Click into the shock detail page]*
>
> "For every shock, we show what happened. The price chart highlights exactly where the shock occurred. Below that — a P&L heatmap inspired by optionsprofitcalculator.com. Green means profit if you fade the move, red means loss. You can see across every possible outcome and time horizon where your edge lives."
>
> *[Hover over the heatmap, show the payoff curve]*
>
> "Payoff curve, scenario analysis, trade simulator — all powered by real backtest data, not estimates. Adjust your position size and everything updates."

---

## ACT 2 — The AI (0:55–1:30)

> "But here's the question a trader actually asks: *why* did this move happen? Is it real news or a panic?"
>
> *[Click "Explain Shock" on the AI Advisor]*
>
> "Our AI agent searches the web in real-time, finds the news that caused the shock, and gives a trade recommendation. It knows that political markets revert at 64% — higher than the overall average — and factors that into its analysis."
>
> *[Show the AI response appearing]*
>
> "And it's smart about context. If a sports market spikes because a team scored late in a close game — that's not an overreaction, that's rational pricing. The agent knows the difference."

---

## ACT 3 — Portfolio Builder (1:30–2:00)

> *[Navigate to the portfolio builder]*
>
> "Real traders don't bet on one market. They build portfolios. Pick your shocks, set your sizes — or let the AI do it."
>
> *[Click "Build with AI"]*
>
> "K2-Think analyzes every available shock, picks the best 3-4 fade candidates based on category win rates, and sizes positions using half-Kelly criterion. You get a full trade memo with rationale for every position."
>
> *[Show the combined payoff graph]*
>
> "The combined payoff graph shows your portfolio's P&L across all scenarios. Diversification across independent markets reduces variance — that's the whole point of a portfolio approach."

---

## ACT 4 — The Chrome Extension (2:00–2:40)

> "Everything I just showed you lives on our dashboard. But traders live on Polymarket. So we brought ShockTest to them."
>
> *[Switch to a Polymarket event page with the extension active]*
>
> "This is Polymarket. See those orange bands on the chart? That's ShockTest. Our Chrome extension intercepts Polymarket's own price data, reads the chart's axis ticks directly from the SVG, and draws shock bands at the exact pixel positions where shocks occurred."
>
> *[Show the floating panel]*
>
> "The panel shows reversion stats for this specific sub-market — not the whole event page, just the one you're looking at. Click through for the full analysis on our dashboard."
>
> *[Switch sub-markets to show bands updating]*
>
> "Switch markets — the bands update. Change the timeframe — they reposition. It feels native because it reads the chart's own data."

---

## CLOSE — The Number (2:40–3:00)

> "We built all of this in 24 hours. Three people, 151 commits."
>
> "The finding is real: **prediction markets overreact, and the overreaction is statistically significant.** ShockTest is the tool that lets you find it, understand it, and trade it."
>
> *[Hold on the dashboard with the key stat visible]*
>
> "ShockTest. Find the edge. Size the trade."

---

## Delivery Notes

- **Leave the live monitor running during the demo.** If a shock fires while judges are watching, mention it. That's the winning moment.
- **Don't rush the Chrome extension.** It's the most visually impressive feature. Let them watch the bands appear on a real Polymarket page.
- **Keep the AI agent call short.** If it takes more than 10 seconds to respond, narrate over it: "While that's searching..." and move to the portfolio builder, then come back.
- **End on the stat, not the tech.** Judges remember the story, not the stack. 59.9% reversion is the headline.
